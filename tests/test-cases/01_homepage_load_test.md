# TC-01: Homepage Load and Navigation

## Open homepage
Go to http://localhost:5000.
Verify the page loads and the SoleStyle logo/text is visible in the header.

## Verify hero section
Assert that a hero banner section is visible on the page with promotional content.

## Verify product grid
Scroll down and assert that product cards are displayed in a grid layout.
Assert that at least 6 product cards are visible.

## Verify navigation links
Assert that the header contains navigation links for Men, Women, Athletic, Casual, Dress, and Sale.

## Verify search bar
Assert that a search input field with placeholder text "Search shoes..." is visible in the header.

## Verify cart icon
Assert that a shopping cart icon is visible in the header area.

## Verify footer
Scroll to the bottom of the page and assert that a footer section is visible.
